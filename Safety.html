The University of Rochester campus is very safe and there are multiple offices that help make sure it stays that way. All around campus there are Blue Light Emergency Phones that connect directly to Public Safety if you're ever in an emergency. Luckily, they are rarely needed.
Public Safety
Public Safety is the go-to on campus for any emergency or safety concern. They also regularly do preventative patrols of roads, public spaces, and residence halls.
MERT
The Medical Emergency Response Team (MERT) is a Basic Life Support, non-transporting EMS agency run by highly trained students on campus.

get rides around campus after the busses stop running
