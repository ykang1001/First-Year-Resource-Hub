The University of Rochester campus is very safe and there are multiple offices that help make sure it stays that way. All around campus there are Blue Light Emergency Phones that connect directly to Public Safety if you're ever in an emergency. Luckily, they are rarely needed.
Public Safety
Public Safety is the go-to on campus for any emergency or safety concern. They also regularly do preventative patrols of roads, public spaces, and residence halls. Their phone number is (585) 275-3333.
MERT
The Medical Emergency Response Team (MERT) is a Basic Life Support, non-transporting EMS agency run by highly trained students on campus.
SafeRide
Use SafeRide to get rides around campus late at night after the busses stop running. Download the TapRide app to request a pick up.
Title IX
The Title IX Office handles all sexual misconduct at UR. There are a variety of ways to report and 24/7 resources on campus if you have been assaulted.
