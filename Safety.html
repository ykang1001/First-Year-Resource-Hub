The University of Rochester campus is very safe and there are multiple offices that help make sure it stays that way. All around campus there are Blue Light Emergency Phones that connect directly to Public Safety if you're ever in an emergency. Luckily, they are rarely needed.

Public Safety

Public Safety is the go-to on campus for any emergency or safety concern. They also regularly do preventative patrols of roads, public spaces, and residence halls. Their phone number is (585) 275-3333 and can be called at any time.
Call Public Safety Public Safety Website

Medical Emergencies

The Medical Emergency Response Team (MERT) is a Basic Life Support, non-transporting EMS agency run by highly trained students on campus. Call Public Safety at any time if you have a medical emergency and MERT will come to assist you. Students often refer to this as "getting MERT-ed."
MERT Website

Late Night Transportation

Use SafeRide to get rides around campus late at night after the busses stop running. Download the TapRide app to request a pick up. Call (585) 276-7433 if the app is not working.
SafeRide Info TapRide (iPhone) TapRide (Android)

Sexual Misconduct

The Title IX Office handles all sexual misconduct at UR. There are a variety of ways to report and 24/7 resources on campus if you have been assaulted. For immediate help call Public Safety or 9-1-1. For confidential resources call UCC (585) 275-3113 or RESTORE (585) 546-2777.
