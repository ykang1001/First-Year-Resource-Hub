University of Rochester has many health services available to students. Use this page to find information about helpful resources and how to use them.
Physical Health
University Health Services (UHS) assigns each student a Primary Care Provider (PCP). Your PCP can help you with any physical needs, from a cold to a sprained ankle. To schedule an appointment, call (585) 275-2662 or walk in to UHS, say you would like to schedule an appointment with your PCP, they will ask your name and birthday to confirm identity, then they find a time for you to come in for an appointment that works with your schedule. You may also have to provide a reason for the visit, which can be as vague or specific as you want. Afterward, you can contact your PCP via UHS Connect, the online messaging and forms system.
